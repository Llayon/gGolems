# Copilot 3D Prompt: Steam-Magic Golem

## Main Prompt

Create a game-ready 3D model of a heavy steam-magic golem for a mech combat game.

The design should feel like a walking boiler-powered war machine built by arcane engineers: part industrial mech, part ritual construct.

Visual direction:
- heavy silhouette, broad shoulders, compact torso, thick legs, oversized feet
- readable from far away, strong mech silhouette first, detail second
- front-facing armored chest with a glowing rune furnace core
- visible boiler, pressure tanks, copper pipes, exhaust stacks, pistons, valves, riveted plates
- mix of brass, bronze, dark iron, soot-stained steel, ceramic heat shielding
- magic components should glow with restrained cyan-blue rune light
- heat and pressure areas can glow warm amber/orange

Shape language:
- torso like an armored furnace or locomotive chest
- arms should be clearly visible and mechanically readable:
  - shoulder block
  - upper arm
  - elbow joint
  - armored forearm
  - simple claw or industrial manipulator hand
- legs should feel weight-bearing and hydraulic:
  - wide hips
  - piston-supported thighs
  - armored shins
  - heavy ankle assembly
  - broad planted feet

Back silhouette:
- large rear boiler and steam plumbing
- twin or triple exhaust chimneys
- pressure vessel rings and maintenance hatches
- arcane rune conduits feeding from furnace to limbs

Head:
- small compared to torso
- not humanoid-cute
- should feel like a command sensor block or armored shrine helm
- include a narrow glowing visor or rune eye cluster

Style target:
- dieselpunk + steampunk + fantasy rune engineering
- heavy and practical, not sleek
- intimidating, ancient-industrial, battlefield-worn

Surface detail:
- rivets
- layered armor plates
- bolted maintenance panels
- engraved runes
- heat discoloration around vents
- soot around stacks
- scratches and battle wear

Avoid:
- anime proportions
- superhero bodybuilder anatomy
- sleek sci-fi plastic surfaces
- modern military tank realism without fantasy
- overly thin limbs
- tiny feet
- decorative clutter that destroys silhouette

Game constraints:
- optimized for real-time game use
- clean readable topology
- separate logical parts for rigging
- symmetrical base model is preferred
- should work in both first-person cockpit game fiction and third-person combat readability

## Optional Technical Follow-Up

If possible, also provide:
- separate material IDs for armor, frame, boiler, rune glow, heat glow
- named moving parts for rigging:
  - pelvis

  - head
  - left_upper_arm

## Short Variant

Heavy steam-magic war golem, readable mech silhouette, brass and dark iron armor, glowing rune furnace core, boiler backpack, exhaust stacks, pistons, broad shoulders, visible mechanical arms, thick hydraulic legs, oversized feet, industrial fantasy design, battle-worn, game-ready, third-person readable.
